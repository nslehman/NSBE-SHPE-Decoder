NSBE-SHPE Member Spotlight
Dana Smith, founder of Lehman's NSBE chapter, has been working with Goldman Sachs for the past two and a half years as a Technology Analyst, developing and maintaining software which prices securities held in Goldman Sachs accounts for institutional wealth clients.

Dana received her Computer Science degree from Lehman College back in 2015, yet her presence here at Lehman's NSBE chapter is still felt. Recently, Dana held a 3 part HackerRank Virtual Training series where she went over HackerRank challenges with our members. Dana also provided our members with feedback on their resumes throughout the duration of the series.

Dana's commitment to the chapter continues to inspire us, and her continued commitment to the advancement of our members' career development is one of the many reasons she is our Spotlight member of the week! Thank you for all that you do, Dana!
