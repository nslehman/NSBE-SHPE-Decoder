NSBE-SHPE Member Spotlight
Scott Tyler has been a member of the NSBE and SHPE chapters at Lehman College since 2016. During Spring 2017, he was part of the Executive Board, and in Fall 2017, he is part of the Hackathon Committee. 

Recently, Scott graduated from MakeSchool. Coming from a Mathematics background, he made a good commitment to learn software development over the summer. During his time at MakeSchool, he discovered his strengths and weaknesses.  He is great at manipulating data and UX, but UI is his weakness. However, he is now ready to work on challenging and exciting projects! 

Currently, Scott is in his third semester teaching Math Courses at Lehman. His journey has been amazing, and he states that, " I think the most valuable part of teaching though is for me, it is also the best way to learn something. If you can teach something in any situation, it will reinforce what you know in addition to helping someone else in their journey."

Scott’s dedication to NSBE-SHPE during his college years and after graduation is one of many reasons he is our Spotlight Member of the week! We at NSBE-SHPE would like to congratulate you and wish you much luck!
